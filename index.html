WHO WE ARE

Clean water is a tremendous issue--it is the number one cause of death and disease in the world according to the World Health Organization. WellPower seeks to address this problem by solving the underlying problem of energy. WellPower uses upcycled solar panels to generate off-grid energy, repurposed electric vehicle battery packs to store the energy, and then uses this energy to run a pump and innovative and proprietary self-cleaning filter. This system meets the water needs of entire communities, and last for 15+ years without any outside intervention or assistance. Due to the innovative sustainable sourcing of the solar panels and batteries, our costs are extremely low and we benefit the environment by reusing tech that would otherwise go to the landfill. Our solution will have a positive social impact by providing people who do have access to clean water or energy with a tool that can clean their water and light their home (which is just one of many energy applications).
